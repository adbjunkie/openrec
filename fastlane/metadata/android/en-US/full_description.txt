OpenRec is a minimal, open-source background video recorder.

<b>Privacy first</b>
• No analytics, ads, or tracking
• No network access
• No cloud upload
• Videos stay on your device

<b>Recording</b>
• Record video while using other apps or with the screen off
• Front or back camera
• Optional audio
• Share or delete recordings from the app

<b>Stealth options</b>
• Change launcher icon and name (Calculator, Notes, Weather, Clock)
• Minimal notification mode (as discreet as Android allows)

<b>Transparency</b>
Android requires a persistent notification while the camera records in the background. OpenRec cannot fully hide this on modern Android, but minimal mode makes it as small and silent as possible.

<b>Legal</b>
You are responsible for complying with local recording consent laws.
